Privacy Policy

At Zonic Marketers, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you in several ways:

  • When you create an account or make a purchase through our online store.
  • When you fill out our contact or feedback forms.
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter or promotional emails.
  • When you browse or interact with our website.

The information we collect may include your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment details.

How We Use Your Information

The information we gather is used to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve your shopping experience and our website functionality.
  • Notify you about new products, promotions, or important updates.
  • Understand customer interests to enhance our product offerings and services.

We may keep track of your preferences and shopping behavior to tailor content and recommend products that suit your needs.

Data Protection & Sharing

We follow strict procedures to ensure your personal information is protected. Your data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties, except when necessary to fulfill orders (e.g., with shipping companies or payment processors).

We do not sell or rent your information to any external organizations.

Postal & Email Communication

We may use your postal address and phone number to:

  • Deliver your orders.
  • Confirm transactions or address order-related issues.
  • Occasionally inform you about new arrivals or special offers (only if you've opted in).

You may also receive periodic emails regarding updates, product launches, or exclusive discounts.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Keep track of items in your shopping cart.
  • Enhance site performance and user experience.
  • Collect anonymous traffic data for analytics purposes.

Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve navigation throughout the site. You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
